LoJack's biglojackarrow.com

In addition to serving as UX Designer on biglojackarrow.com, I also produced the digital offerings of the campaign, which included online banners, a microsite that served as a hub for the campaign as well as a contest entry point, and finally a service to enter by SMS.

People were driven to the site by the online banner ads, but more importantly from our street teams roaming LoJack's 4 biggest markets in cars with 6 foot flashing arrows on top, demonstrating LoJack's ability to locate vehicles.
